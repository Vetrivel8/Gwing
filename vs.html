About

Founded in 1905, is a Swiss luxury watchmaker renowned for precision, durability, and timeless design. Known for innovations like the waterproof Oyster case (1926) and self-winding Perpetual movement (1931), Rolex produces watches certified as Superlative Chronometers for accuracy. Crafted from premium materials such as Oystersteel, 18k gold, and scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, Rolex offers iconic collections like the Submariner, Daytona, and Datejust. Designed for professionals and adventurers, they excel in functionality and elegance. Rolex watches symbolize prestige and reliability, making them highly sought-after in both the luxury and collector markets.
